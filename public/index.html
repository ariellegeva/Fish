The Rules of Fish 🐟

You are on a team with every other person in the circle — teams alternate.

You want to collect the cards in each half suit.

Half Suits

Each half suit has six cards. There are no 8s or Jokers in the game.

  • ♥ 2 3 4 5 6 7 = Low Hearts
  • ♥ 9 10 J Q K A = High Hearts
  • Same for clubs, diamonds, and spades.

Asking

  • If you ask someone for a card and they have it, they give it to you and you get another turn.
  • If they don't have it, it becomes their turn.
  • You can only ask members of the opposite team.
  • You can only ask for cards in a half suit that you already hold a card from.
  • You cannot ask for a card you already hold.
  • If you're out of cards you can still claim, but can't ask. If it's still your turn you can pass to a teammate.

Claiming

  • You can claim when it is your team's turn.
  • You should only claim if you think all 6 cards in a half suit are on one team.
  • When claiming, state which player you think has each of the 6 cards.
  • If correct → your team gets the suit (1 point).
  • If right team but wrong positions → 0 points, suit goes to the middle.
  • If wrong team has a card → opposite team gets the suit.
  • Once you start a claim you must follow through.
  • You cannot communicate with your team during a claim.

Communicating with your team

You can only communicate through the cards you ask for — by repeatedly asking the same question or pointedly blocking out a player.
